How this is worked out
  • Tax saved = the VC escapes income tax when deducted from payroll. Pension contributions remain an eligible deduction under the Nigeria Tax Act, 2025 (effective 1 January 2026), which consolidated the former Personal Income Tax Act.
  • Income assumes annual compounding at your expected return, pro-rated for the holding period.
  • Withdrawal: half of (contribution + income) is accessible from 91 days after contributing.
  • Withdrawal tax: the income portion you take out is taxed if withdrawn under 12 months from contribution; from 12 months on, it is tax-free.
